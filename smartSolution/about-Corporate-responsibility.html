Our Global Impact

Since 2009, financial institutions using Universa have collectively contributed billions of dollars in economic activity worldwide. Universa software is being used in Canada, in the Caribbean, in Central America and plans to expand its impact further afield are already on the board.

Being a standard browser-based platform makes Universa globally accessible which is indispensable in today’s fast-paced 24/7 financial world.

We help to ensure that people needing to make a financial transaction can do so with speed and efficiency regardless of where they are located on the globe.

Commitment to Sustainability

Sustainability focuses on meeting the needs of today without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their needs. It champions a variety of new deliverables for corporations to pursue and helps them reassess existing commitments to basic goals like efficiency, productivity, sustainable growth and new talent acquisition.

Smart Solution’s commitment to its short-, intermediate- and long-term goals define its strategy to provide its clients with an ongoing excellence of service, now as well as in the future.

Our Focus
